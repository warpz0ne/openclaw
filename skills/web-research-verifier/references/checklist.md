# Research Checklist

## Intake
- Exact question and decision to support
- Geography and timezone
- Freshness requirement (today / last 30 days / evergreen)
- Required depth (quick scan vs deep dive)

## Source Quality Ladder
1. Primary: official vendor/regulator/docs/pricing/legal text
2. Secondary: reputable reporting and analyst notes
3. Tertiary: forums/social (context only, never sole basis)

## Minimum Evidence Standard
- >=3 independent sources for major conclusions
- >=1 primary source for each critical claim
- Explicit note for any unresolved conflict

## Conflict Handling
- Record conflicting values side-by-side
- Identify likely reason (date mismatch, plan tier difference, region)
- Recommend verification action

## Pricing-Specific Checks
- Currency and tax inclusion
- Intro promo vs renewal
- Monthly vs annual billing assumptions
- Usage-based components (storage, egress, seats, requests)
- Hidden mandatory costs

## Final QA Before Sending
- Are all key claims cited?
- Are dates included where relevant?
- Are assumptions explicit?
- Is uncertainty clearly labeled?
- Is recommendation decision-oriented?
